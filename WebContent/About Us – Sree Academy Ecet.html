Welcome to Sree Academy

SREE Academy was established in the year 2011 with a prime motto of imparting quality education in engineering and moulding the POLYTECHNIC students to crack competitive AND PUBLIC SECTOR examinations. From a small coaching centre with just a handful of students in the year 2011 BORN IN SIDDIPET, the institute has grown by leaps and bounds and established itself as one of the leading institutions in TELANGANA  for ECET/POLYCET coaching

MANAGEMENT
